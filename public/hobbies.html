My Favorite Hobbies

What I do in my free time:

  1. Reading

    • I love to read both novels and manga (Japanese comics.)
    • My favorite genres are:
      • Horror
      • Slice of Life
      • Psychological/ Thriller
      • Romance

  2. Creating

    • My favorite things are drawing and creative writing.
    • Even though I'm not the best at either, I still love to craft stories and create characters.

  3. Cooking

    • Eating good food is something that I don't think I could live without.
    • Testing new recipes is something I do almost every week, I think I would get bored if I ate the same things all of the time.
    • Baking is another aspect of cooking I do a lot, particularly cakes, cookies, breads, and pies.

  4. Camping

    • My favorite place to go camping is the Adirondack Mountains.
    • While camping, I love to go hiking, biking, and canoeing.
    • My dream is to be able to take a road trip around the United States and camp in as many places as I can.
